Quest 2

The Meta Quest 2 (formly known as OculusQuest 2)is the best VR headset to buy for
most people. It offers the most versatility and the best value in the most affordable package
available.

As mentioned above, the Quest 2 is powered a Qualcomm SnapDragon XR-2 SoC,a
processor and graghics chip derived from the company's flagship smartphone processors but
optimized for VR and AR capabilities. The Quest 2 is a self-contained device that does not
tether or a Wi-Ficonnection, giving you the best of both worlds.

Quest 2 offers a high-resolution VR experience without the need for a computer. The headset
provide 1832 x 1920 pixels per eye, a higher resolution than the Valve Index and HTC Vive
Cosmos tethered PC headset.Quest 2 also offers variable refresh rate, with developer able
to targer 60hz, 90hz, and 120hz configurations depending on the performence demands of
the games.

Meta Quest 2 is currently available in a 128GB entry model and a larger 256GB model.The
original 64GB edition is no longer sold. If you finde one the shelf, we recommended leaving
it unless it is an outstanding deal. Some of the newest Quest 2 games barely fit on the smaller
cover for better hygiene.
